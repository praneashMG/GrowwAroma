Treasures of the Earth

We source our raw materials from the finest harvests around the globe, ensuring unparalleled quality in every drop.

Grasse, France

Centifolia Rose

Harvested by hand at dawn in the fields of Grasse, our rose absolute is renowned for its honeyed, sensual depth. It takes tens of thousands of petals to produce a single ounce of this precious nectar.

Calabria, Italy

Sparkling Bergamot

Providing the bright, effervescent opening to our fresh collections, our bergamot is cold-pressed from the rinds of sun-ripened fruit in southern Italy, offering a complex, slightly floral citrus profile.

Assam, India

Agarwood (Oud)

Known as "liquid gold," our sustainable oud resin provides a mysterious, smoky, and profoundly woody base. It anchors our evening fragrances with a luxurious, lingering intensity.
