The Anatomy of a Fragrance

A perfume is like a symphony. Discover how a fragrance evolves on your skin from the first spray to the lingering memory.

1. Top Notes

0 - 15 MINUTES

The initial impression. Light, fresh, and sharp ingredients like bright citrus and crushed herbs that evaporate quickly but capture your immediate attention.

2. Heart Notes

15 - 60 MINUTES

The core of the fragrance. As top notes fade, rich florals, warm spices, and green notes emerge to form the true personality and soul of the scent.

3. Base Notes

1 - 24 HOURS

The lasting memory. Heavy, molecularly dense ingredients like deep woods, sensual musk, and rich vanilla that anchor the fragrance to your skin.
